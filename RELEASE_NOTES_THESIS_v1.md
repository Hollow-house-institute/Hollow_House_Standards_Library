# The Execution-Time Governance Gap: Decision Boundaries, Interaction Traces, and Runtime Accountability in AI Systems

This release publishes the first standards-grade execution-time governance thesis from the Hollow House Institute.

The publication examines the operational gap between governance obligations and runtime enforcement capability across AI systems.

Core operational areas include:
- Decision Boundary enforcement
- Governance Telemetry
- Interaction Trace continuity
- Stop Authority
- Escalation controls
- Continuous Assurance
- Longitudinal Accountability

The thesis positions governance as runtime operational infrastructure rather than post-hoc documentation.

Key operational failure conditions examined:
- Governance Drift
- Accountability Diffusion
- Decision Substitution
- Override Erosion
- escalation failure
- fragmented audit evidence
