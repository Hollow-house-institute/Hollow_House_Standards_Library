# Behavioral AI Governance Terminology

The HHI Governance Glossary defines canonical terminology used in Behavioral AI Governance and Execution-Time Governance systems.

This vocabulary provides structured definitions for governance concepts including:

- Governance Infrastructure
- Authority Structures
- Behavioral Drift
- Longitudinal Risk
- Execution-Time Governance
- Human–AI Interaction Dynamics

Canonical sources:

- glossary.md
- glossary.json
- TERMS.md
- TERMS_INDEX.md

The glossary is maintained as a machine-readable registry and a human-readable specification to ensure semantic stability across governance systems.
