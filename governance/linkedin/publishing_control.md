# HHI LinkedIn Publishing Control

## Decision Boundary
Publish only if:
- canonical HHI terms are used exactly
- claims are framed as observable patterns, not unsupported facts
- Governance Telemetry can be captured
- Accountability is assigned

## Escalation
Escalate to Human-in-the-Loop if:
- terminology drift appears twice
- comments show repeated misunderstanding
- Authority Alignment Score declines across two posts

Escalation persists until corrected.

## Stop Authority
Stop publishing if:
- canonical terms are paraphrased
- Decision Boundary is missing
- Governance Telemetry is not captured
- Accountability is undefined

## Accountability
Owner: Hollow House Institute
Validator: Human-in-the-Loop
System Role: enforce pre-publication checks

## Governance Telemetry
Event | Actor | Decision Boundary | Action | Outcome | Escalation | Timestamp
