# HHI-TERM-03
## Terminology Governance Standard

Status: Draft

Purpose:
Establish governance controls for canonical terminology across the Hollow House Institute ecosystem.

Objectives:

- Preserve terminology continuity
- Detect terminology drift
- Maintain canonical definitions
- Support replayable evidence
- Enable longitudinal accountability

Authority:
Human authority remains final authority.

Core Controls:

1. Canonical Registry
2. Drift Detection
3. Coverage Measurement
4. Lineage Tracking
5. Term Approval Workflow
6. Deprecation Workflow

Evidence Outputs:

- TERM_AUDIT_REPORT
- TERM_DRIFT_REPORT
- TERM_COVERAGE_REPORT
- TERM_LINEAGE_REPORT
