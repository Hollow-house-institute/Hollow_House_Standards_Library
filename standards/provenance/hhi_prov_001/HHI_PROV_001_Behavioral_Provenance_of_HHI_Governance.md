# HHI_PROV_001: Behavioral Provenance of HHI Governance

Institution: Hollow House Institute
Founder: Amy Pierce Bui

## Origin Thesis

The Hollow House Institute governance architecture did not originate from abstract theory.

It emerged from repeated observation that declarations, intentions, policies, and promises often diverge from actual behavior.

The foundational insight became:

Behavior is the most honest data there is.

From that observation emerged Behavioral Governance, Execution-Time Governance, Governance Telemetry, Replayable Evidence, Longitudinal Accountability, and Governance Observability.

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.
