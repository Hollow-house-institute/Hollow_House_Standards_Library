# HHI-METRIC-01

## Governance Measurement Framework

Status: Draft

### Purpose

Establish canonical measurement methods for governance systems.

### Measurement Domains

- Governance Health
- Behavioral Drift
- Authority Alignment
- Governance Telemetry
- Governance Observability
- Continuous Assurance

### Core Principles

- What cannot be observed cannot be governed.
- What cannot be measured cannot be improved.
- Measurements must be replayable.
- Measurements must be auditable.
- Human authority remains final authority.

### Candidate Metrics

- Governance Health Score
- Behavioral Drift Score
- Authority Alignment Score
- Assurance Coverage Score
- Observability Coverage Score
- Telemetry Completeness Score

### Open Questions

- Metric weighting
- Drift thresholds
- Escalation thresholds
- Confidence calculation
- Longitudinal scoring
