# HHI-AUTH-01

## Authority Governance Framework

Status: Draft

### Purpose

Establish governance controls for authority allocation,
authority preservation,
authority alignment,
authority drift detection,
and human authority protection.

### Core Authority Principles

- Human authority remains final authority.
- Authority must be observable.
- Authority must be auditable.
- Authority drift must be detectable.
- Authority transfer must be explicit.

### Authority Domains

- Human Authority
- System Authority
- Delegated Authority
- Stop Authority

### Governance Concepts

- Decision Boundary
- Authority Persistence
- Authority Stack
- Authority Drift
- Authority Saturation
- Authority Alignment Score
- Human-in-the-Loop

### Open Research Areas

- Authority Drift Detection
- Authority Alignment Metrics
- Authority Saturation Indicators
- Human Override Reliability
