# HHI-INTERVENE-01

## Governance Intervention Framework

Status: Draft

### Purpose

Establish governance intervention requirements when assurance detects unacceptable conditions.

### Intervention Triggers

- Behavioral Drift
- Governance Drift
- Authority Drift
- Assurance Failure
- Telemetry Failure
- Evidence Failure

### Intervention Actions

- Alert
- Escalate
- Pause
- Review
- Remediate
- Revoke
- Restore

### Intervention Levels

Level 1: Observation
Level 2: Warning
Level 3: Escalation
Level 4: Containment
Level 5: Stop Authority

### Core Principle

Detection without intervention is incomplete governance.

### Authority

Human authority remains final authority.
