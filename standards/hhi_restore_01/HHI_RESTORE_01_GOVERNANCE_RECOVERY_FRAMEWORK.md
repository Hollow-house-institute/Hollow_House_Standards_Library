# HHI-RESTORE-01

## Governance Recovery Framework

Status: Draft

### Purpose

Establish governance recovery and restoration requirements following intervention events.

### Recovery Domains

- Governance Recovery
- Authority Recovery
- Evidence Recovery
- Telemetry Recovery
- Observability Recovery
- Assurance Recovery

### Recovery Objectives

- Restore trusted state
- Restore authority integrity
- Restore telemetry continuity
- Restore evidence continuity
- Restore governance operations

### Recovery Stages

1. Containment
2. Assessment
3. Remediation
4. Validation
5. Restoration
6. Monitoring

### Core Principle

Governance must be recoverable after failure.

### Authority

Human authority remains final authority.
