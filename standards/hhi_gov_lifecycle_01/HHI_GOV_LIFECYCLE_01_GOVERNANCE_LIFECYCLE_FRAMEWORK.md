# HHI-GOV-LIFECYCLE-01

## Governance Lifecycle Framework

Status: Draft

### Purpose

Establish the canonical operational lifecycle for governance systems.

### Lifecycle Stages

1. Authority
2. Measurement
3. Observability
4. Assurance
5. Intervention
6. Recovery

### Stage Relationships

Authority
  ↓
Measurement
  ↓
Observability
  ↓
Assurance
  ↓
Intervention
  ↓
Recovery

### Inputs

- Governance Events
- Governance Telemetry
- Audit Evidence
- Authority Decisions
- Runtime Signals

### Outputs

- Governance Status
- Assurance Status
- Intervention Actions
- Recovery Actions
- Replayable Evidence

### Handoff Conditions

Authority → Measurement
- Authority defined

Measurement → Observability
- Metrics available

Observability → Assurance
- Signals observable

Assurance → Intervention
- Failure detected

Intervention → Recovery
- Corrective action initiated

Recovery → Authority
- Trusted state restored

### Core Principle

Governance is a continuous operational lifecycle rather than a static policy artifact.

### Authority

Human authority remains final authority.
