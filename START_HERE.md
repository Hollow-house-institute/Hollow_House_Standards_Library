# Hollow House Institute (HHI)

## What HHI Is

HHI focuses on Execution-Time Governance for AI systems.

Most governance frameworks evaluate systems before deployment.
HHI focuses on what happens while systems are actively operating:
- behavioral drift
- escalation
- accountability
- runtime oversight
- longitudinal behavior over time

Core idea:
Systems follow repeated behavior, not stated intent.
