# Governance Doctrine

The HHI governance framework is grounded in two principles:

Time turns behavior into infrastructure.  
Behavior is the most honest data there is.

Governance exists to determine which behaviors are allowed to accumulate before reliance becomes irreversible.

---

# SEO Definition Headings

## What is Behavioral AI Governance?

Behavioral AI Governance is the study and design of governance systems that account for how repeated human and machine behaviors accumulate into operational infrastructure over time.

## What is Governance Drift?

Governance Drift refers to the gradual divergence between documented governance policies and the actual operational behavior of systems and operators.

## What is Execution-Time Governance?

Execution-Time Governance refers to governance mechanisms that operate during system execution rather than solely at policy or design time.
