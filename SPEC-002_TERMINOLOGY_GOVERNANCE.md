# SPEC-002
## Terminology Governance & Canonicalization

Document ID: HHI-SPEC-002  
Version: 1.0.0  
Status: Canonical  

---

## Purpose
This specification governs how terms are created, validated, and stabilized.

---

## Scope
Applies to all glossary, audit, and framework terminology.

---

## Core Rule
Terms must follow structured coining, validation, and canonicalization.

---

## Enforcement
Any non-compliant term constitutes Governance Drift.
