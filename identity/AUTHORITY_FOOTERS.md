# Hollow House Institute Canonical Authority Footers

## Purpose

This document defines the canonical public authority footers used across Hollow House Institute (HHI) repositories, governance artifacts, publications, audit exports, LinkedIn posts, DEV articles, release notes, and public operational governance materials.

These footer structures exist to preserve:

- identity continuity
- authority continuity
- repository hierarchy consistency
- replayable attribution
- longitudinal discoverability
- governance provenance integrity
