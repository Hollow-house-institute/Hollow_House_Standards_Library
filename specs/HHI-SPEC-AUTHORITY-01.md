## 1. Real System Behavior Observation

## 2. Canonical Governance Term
**Authority**  
**Identifier:** HHI-AUTH-002

## 3. Exact Glossary Definition
**Authority** — "The formally assigned right to make, override, pause, or stop decisions during execution."

## 4. Behavioral Impact
Authority determines who can act, intervene, or halt system behavior.  
When misaligned, it leads to:

- Decision Substitution  
- Authority Drift  
- Accountability Diffusion  

## 5. Connection to Governance Infrastructure
Authority is a structural pillar of **Governance Infrastructure**, defining who holds final decision rights during **Execution-Time Governance**.
