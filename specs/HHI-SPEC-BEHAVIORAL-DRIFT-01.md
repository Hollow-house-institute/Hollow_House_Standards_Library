## 1. Real System Behavior Observation
An AI system gradually begins producing outputs that differ from its original behavioral profile, even though no model update occurred.

## 2. Canonical Governance Term
**Behavioral Drift**  
**Identifier:** HHI-BEH-001

## 3. Exact Glossary Definition
**Behavioral Drift** — "Gradual divergence between expected system behavior and observed behavior over time."

## 4. Behavioral Impact
Behavioral Drift is the primary mechanism through which systems accumulate unintended influence.  
It drives:

- Governance Drift  
- Reliance Formation  
- Judgment Externalization  

## 5. Connection to Governance Infrastructure
Behavioral Drift is a core signal in **Governance Telemetry** and must be monitored continuously through **Execution-Time Governance**.
