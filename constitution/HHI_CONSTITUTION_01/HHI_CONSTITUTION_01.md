# HHI-CONSTITUTION-01

## Constitutional Primitives

HHI-CP-001 Behavioral Substrate
Behavior is the root of governance reality.

HHI-CP-002 Authority Origin
Authority must have a legitimate and traceable source.

HHI-CP-003 Boundary Integrity
Boundaries constrain operation and prevent drift.

HHI-CP-004 Replayability
Governance must be reconstructable.

HHI-CP-005 Continuity of Identity
A governance system must remain itself over time.

HHI-CP-006 Evidence Discipline
No governance claim without evidence.

HHI-CP-007 Transparency of Process
Governance must be inspectable.

HHI-CP-008 Terminological Stability
Meaning must remain stable across time.
