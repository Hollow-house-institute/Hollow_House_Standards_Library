# HHI Governance Taxonomy

This taxonomy defines the classification of governance artifacts within the Hollow House Standards Library.

## Category: Glossary Artifacts
Artifacts defining governance terminology.

- glossary.md — Human-readable glossary
- glossary.json — Machine-readable glossary
- glossary.schema.json — JSON Schema defining glossary structure

## Category: Authority Artifacts
Artifacts defining semantic authority and boundaries.

- AUTHORITY.md

## Category: Structural Governance Artifacts
Artifacts defining the architecture and relationships of governance components.

- GOVERNANCE_ARCHITECTURE.md
- GOVERNANCE_TAXONOMY.md
- GOVERNANCE_ONTOLOGY.md
- GOVERNANCE_STANDARDS_CROSSWALK.md

## Category: Integrity Artifacts
Artifacts ensuring reproducibility and drift detection.

- CHECKSUMS.sha256
- CANONICAL_CHECKSUMS.txt
- GLOSSARY_SHA256.txt
- LOCK_MANIFEST.md
- CHANGELOG.md
