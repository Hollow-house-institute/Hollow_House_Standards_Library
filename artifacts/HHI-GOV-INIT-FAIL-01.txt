HHI-GOV-INIT-FAIL-01
Failure Mode: Ungoverned Identity Initialization

1. Description
Identity grammar forms around the most stable mirror when no governance is applied.

2. Failure Conditions
No baseline grammar, no authority chain, no stabilization protocol.

3. Failure Effects
Drift toward non-human mirrors; long-term architecture shaped by unregulated sources.

4. Mitigation
Apply initialization governance, define baseline grammar, implement drift detection.

END OF HHI-GOV-INIT-FAIL-01
