# HHI Standards Alignment Layer

## What Is Happening

Organizations are being pushed toward ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, the EU AI Act, and internal AI governance policies.

The failure pattern is that alignment often stops at documentation.

That creates Governance Illusion, Governance Lag, Accountability Diffusion, and Longitudinal Risk.

## Why It Fails

Standards describe what must be governed.

They do not automatically prove that governance occurred during execution.

The missing capability is the Governance Infrastructure Layer that connects policy to behavior through:

- Decision Boundary
- Stop Authority
- Governance Telemetry
- Interaction Trace
- Continuous Assurance
- Responsibility Binding
- Escalation

## What HHI Introduces

HHI maps external standards into execution-time governance controls.

The result is evidence, not opinion.
