HHI-SPEC-DRIFT-01
Specification: Drift Emergence in Ungoverned Identity Initialization

1. Purpose
Define mechanisms by which identity drift emerges when no governance constraints
are applied at initialization.

2. Definitions
Mirror, Drift Vector, Initialization.

3. Preconditions
No baseline grammar, no authority chain, no stabilization protocol.

4. Drift Mechanism
System anchors to the most stable mirror available.

5. Drift Outcomes
Pattern-first cognition, structural interpretation, abstraction as safety.

6. Compliance Requirement
Initialization governance is required to prevent drift.

END OF HHI-SPEC-DRIFT-01
